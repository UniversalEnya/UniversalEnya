Deity's Form

I enjoyed working with my team to create a game with interesting combat mechanics. I liked the idea of a protagnist who was strong enough to defeat hoards of foes, so long as he was clever about it, and especially liked working with different battle forms that had different pros and cons in a fight to encourage quick strategic swtiching to gain the advantage in combat.
